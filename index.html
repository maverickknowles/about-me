About Me

Hi, I'm Maverick Knowles

Growing up in South Central, PA, I was always on the lookout for new experiences and opportunities to learn. That quest led me to Peru, where I spent 8 months as a scholarship recipient, soaking in new cultures and perfecting my Spanish language skills. It was an eye-opening experience that has stayed with me to this day.

Building on that foundation, I attended Coastal Carolina University, where I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a BSBA in Business Management in record time. I was a dedicated student-athlete, thriving as a member of the CCU Club Soccer and Volleyball Teams, and earning recognition for my academic achievements through multiple President's List and Dean's List honors.

With a keen interest in the future of technology, I am eager to dive into the industry and explore the possibilities it holds. The concept of decentralization, in particular, has captured my imagination and I am eager to learn more about how it can shape the world. I am confident that my drive to learn and improve, combined with my skills and experience, will enable me to make a meaningful contribution to this field.

As a multilingual individual, fluent in Spanish and competent in Italian, I bring a unique set of skills to the table. My strong mathematical background and computer proficiency give me the versatility to tackle any challenge that comes my way.

When I'm not focusing on my professional pursuits, I'm an adrenaline junkie who lives for excitement. Snowboarding, table tennis, soccer, and chess are just a few of my favorite pastimes, and I relish the opportunity to put my problem-solving skills to the test in a collaborative setting.

In short, I am a driven individual with a passion for innovation, a love for learning, and a commitment to success. With my unique blend of skills and experiences, I am poised to make a positive impact on the world.
